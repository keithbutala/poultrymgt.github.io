ABOUT APP

Be incharge of your poultry

Why fowlX?

  • Its a digital farm management application
  • Aimed at optimizing farming processes
  • Has a number of smart features available
  • Aids farmers to meet the continuously growing demand of chicken

Main Importance

It keeps track of:
  • Egg collection
  • Bird population
  • Vaccinations and treatments
  • Keeps track of stock levels(poultry feeds)
  • Data's concerning bird motarlity is maintained in our systems

Overview

  • FowlX is an easy to use and implement technology
  • Enables farmers to manage every aspect of their poultry farm
  • Monitors variety of chicken breeds ie; broilers and layers
  • System provides remote and real-time monitoring
  • Features an intuitive dashboard display
  • Application designed to get records of all transactions and activities in a poultry farm
  • Integrates daily reports, sales, purchases, etc
  • Estimates feed formulation depending on the breed and stage of the birds and displays the balance
  • Sets reminders for vaccination for different flocks accoding to age
  • Monitors trend of sales and displays profit/loss curve for easy decision making

Major Benefits of FowlX

  • Famers will be updated when its necessary to purchase supplies
  • Farmers will have an advanced and efficient record keeping system
  • Farmers will have a clear overview of their sales thus placed in a better financial position
  • Farmers will always be notified about the regular vaccination schedules, deworming and check-up of the birds
